Odoo - POS Hide/Show Invoice Button

Full control at your fingertips — hide or show POS invoice buttons instantly.

POS Hide/Show Invoice Button for Odoo is a powerful yet simple tool to control invoice button visibility in your POS system. With this module, you can easily hide, show, or restrict the invoice button according to your business workflow. This helps prevent invoicing errors, speeds up checkout, and keeps the POS interface clean. Whether you want to allow only managers to create invoices or remove the invoice option completely, this module gives you the flexibility you need — all without technical skills. Perfect for businesses looking to streamline POS operations and improve cashier efficiency.

POS Hide/Show Invoice Button Workflow

  • Install the POS Hide/Show Invoice Buttons module in your Odoo system.
  • Go to Point of Sale > Settings and scroll to the POS Invoice Button Control section.
  • Enable the Hide Invoice Button option and save the settings.
  • Start a new POS session from the POS dashboard.
  • Create a POS order by adding products to the cart and proceed to the payment screen.
  • You will see that the Invoice button is hidden on the payment screen.
  • To show the invoice button again, go back to POS Settings, disable the Hide Invoice Button option, and save.
  • Start a fresh POS session, and the Invoice button will now be visible on the payment screen.

Hide Invoice Button - POS Settings

Navigate to Point of Sale → Settings, scroll to the POS Hide/Show Invoice Button section, and disable the Hide Invoice Button option. Save the settings to hide the invoice button on the payment screen.

Invoice Button Visible - Payment Screen

When the Hide Invoice Button option is disabled in POS settings, the Invoice button will be clearly visible on the payment screen, allowing cashiers to create invoices instantly.

Activate Hide Invoice Button - POS Settings

Go to Point of Sale → Settings, enable the Hide Invoice Button option, and save the settings. This will hide the invoice button from the POS payment screen.

Invoice Button Hidden - Payment Screen

After enabling the Hide Invoice Button option in POS settings, the Invoice button will no longer appear on the payment screen, keeping the interface clean and distraction-free.
