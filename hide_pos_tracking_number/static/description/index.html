Hide Tracking Number on Receipt

Hide tracking numbers on POS receipts instantly with a single backend toggle.

The Hide Tracking Number on Receipt module for Odoo 18 enables businesses to seamlessly manage the display of tracking numbers on POS receipts. With a simple backend toggle in the POS settings, users can choose to hide or display tracking numbers instantly, ensuring clean and professional-looking receipts. Fully integrated without modifying core templates, this lightweight module provides a flexible, user-friendly solution for enhancing the POS experience and maintaining operational clarity.

Hide Tracking Number on Receipt Workflow

  • Install the POS Hide Tracking Number module in Odoo.
  • Go to POS Settings and locate the POS Hide Tracking Number on Receipt section.
  • Enable the Hide Tracking Number toggle to hide tracking numbers on POS receipts.
  • Start a POS session from the POS dashboard.
  • When the toggle is enabled, all POS receipts will hide tracking numbers automatically.
  • If the toggle is disabled, tracking numbers will be displayed normally on POS receipts.
  • Continue processing POS orders as usual, with receipt display controlled by the backend setting.
